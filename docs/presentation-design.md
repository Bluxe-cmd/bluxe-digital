# Presentation & Visual Design Cheat Sheet

A quick, high-fidelity guide for building decks and one-pagers that match the B. Luxe Digital brand.

## Brand feel
- **Voice:** Warm mentor energy—faith-rooted, direct, encouraging (“sis,” “friend” sparingly).
- **Mood:** Premium, calm, focused. Avoid clutter and harsh contrasts.
- **Imagery:** Confident women of color, notebooks, quiet workspace moments, light bokeh textures.

## Core visual system
- **Palette (Tailwind-friendly):**
  - Primary: Purple 600 `#7C3AED` / Purple 700 `#6B21A8`
  - Accent: Rose 400 `#FB7185`
  - Neutrals: Stone 50 `#FAFAF9`, Stone 200 `#E7E5E4`, Stone 900 `#1C1917`
- **Typography:** Bold display for headings (e.g., Inter/Manrope/SF Pro), medium weight body copy. Letterspacing +2% on headings for sophistication.
- **Shapes:** Rounded 16–24px corners; pill buttons; soft gradients (purple 600 → purple 800).
- **Spacing:** 12pt / 16pt baseline grid, generous white space around quotes and CTAs.

## Slide patterns to reuse
- **Opening framing:** Title + 1-line promise + muted background image with purple gradient overlay.
- **Credibility:** 3–4 key proof points in cards with subtle drop shadow (`shadow-lg/10` style).
- **Problem → Transformation:** Two-column “Before / After” with icons; limit text to 3 bullets each.
- **Product spotlight:** 3D mock (or cover) on left, benefits list on right, price/tagline chip.
- **Call to action:** Solid purple background, white text, single button + backup link.

## Microcopy guardrails
- Avoid jargon; write as if coaching one person.
- Lead with outcomes, then describe features.
- End slides with a question or action (“Ready to map your Valley Season?”).

## Quick template (6 slides)
1. **Title:** Promise + subtitle
2. **Context:** The struggle (2–3 bullets)
3. **Transformation:** Before/After grid
4. **Offer:** Product or bundle with price and who it’s for
5. **Social proof:** Testimonial or stat
6. **CTA:** Single next step (chat link or product URL)

## Checklist before delivering
- Consistent padding and alignment; no orphaned words.
- Contrast passes WCAG for text on gradients.
- Logos clear at 24–32px height; never stretched.
- Export at 16:9, 1920×1080; use PNG for crisp edges, PDF for sharing.
