## Weekly Rhythm

### Monday (30 min — Bianca solo or with Ops)
- Review funding pipeline: What's due this week? What needs follow-up?
- Review outreach tracker: Who responded? Who needs a follow-up?
- Set 3 priorities for the week (no more than 3 — execute these, don't scatter)

### Wednesday (1 hour — team call if applicable)
- Business development calls / meetings (schedule external calls on Wednesdays)
- Partnership conversations
- Foundation / government check-ins

### Friday (30 min — Bianca + Ops)
- Metrics review (see dashboard below)
- Email outreach batch (Fridays are good for outreach — people read over the weekend)
- Week recap + blockers
- Update status in master tracker

---

## Monthly Rhythm

### First Monday of Month (1.5 hours — Bianca + leadership)
- Financial review: Revenue vs. budget, burn rate, cash position
- Funding pipeline review: Applications in progress, expected decisions
- CBA/partnership pipeline: Active conversations, stage, next steps
- Curriculum/operations: Cohort status, facilitator performance
- Marketing/comms: What went out, what's planned

### Second or Third Week of Month
- Board or advisor call (once entity/board is formed)
- Community Advisory Council call (quarterly, not monthly)
- External partnership check-ins (college, key employers)

## Tools Stack (Recommended)

### Communications
- Email: Gmail (Workspace) — equitai.org domain
- Video calls: Zoom or Google Meet
- Team messaging: Slack (free tier works for small team)

### Project/Task Management
- Asana or Notion (free tier for small team)
- Alternatively: Google Sheets tracker (template above)
- Bianca's personal task tracking: Daily 3 priorities in a notebook or Notion

### Financial
- Accounting: QuickBooks Simple Start ($15/mo) or Wave (free)
- Invoicing: Wave, QuickBooks, or Stripe
- Bank: Relay (free, good for small business banking) or M&T Bank
- Payroll (when hiring): Gusto ($40/mo base) or ADP

### CRM (Contact Relationship Management)
- HubSpot Free (good for small team — tracks contacts, deals, emails)
- Alternatively: Airtable with a contacts template
- Minimum viable: Google Sheets with the tracker above

### Document Management
- Google Drive (organized by function: Funding / Operations / Curriculum / Legal)
- Shared with team members by folder level

### Grant Management
- Submittable (some foundations require this)
- Fluxx (for larger foundation portals)
- Think about creating a dedicated grants@equitai.org email for application submissions

## Insurance (Required Before Operations)

Before delivering any programs or signing contracts, ensure EquitAI has:

| Insurance Type | Purpose | Estimated Annual Cost |
|---------------|---------|----------------------|
| General Liability | Property damage, personal injury at program sites | $500–$1,500 |
| Professional Liability (E&O) | Claims about program quality or outcomes | $800–$2,000 |
| Directors & Officers (if 501(c)(3)) | Board liability protection | $1,000–$2,500 |
| Workers Comp | If hiring any employees | Required by MD law; variable |

**Resources:**
- NEXT Insurance (small business online): nextinsurance.com
- Hiscox (small nonprofit): hiscox.com
- Ask your Maryland attorney for recommendations
