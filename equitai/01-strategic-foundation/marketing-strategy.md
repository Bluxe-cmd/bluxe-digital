# EquitAI Marketing Strategy — Year 1

**Version:** 1.0 | March 2026 | Owner: Marketing Lead

---

## Marketing Objectives (Year 1)

1. Establish EquitAI as Maryland's premier AI literacy workforce solution
2. Generate 200+ qualified leads (employers, government, schools, adult learners)
3. Sign 6 pilot sites by Month 4
4. Secure media coverage in 3+ Maryland outlets
5. Build email list to 2,000+ subscribers
6. Position Bianca as Maryland's go-to voice on equitable AI workforce development

---

## Target Audiences and Channels

### Audience 1: Workforce Development Directors (Priority)
**Who:** MDOL, MEC, community college workforce offices, county economic development
**Where:** LinkedIn, email, in-person conferences (Maryland Workforce Association)
**Message:** AI workforce gap + EquitAI solution + government contract pathway
**Tactic:** Executive briefing documents + Bianca speaking at workforce events

### Audience 2: Data Center Developers and Their Community Relations Teams
**Who:** QTS, Equinix, JLL, COPT, Meta, Microsoft (Maryland data centers)
**Where:** Direct email + LinkedIn + commercial real estate events
**Message:** CBA compliance + talent pipeline = one EquitAI partnership
**Tactic:** 1-page CBA partnership brief + intro call with community relations VP

### Audience 3: High School Students and Parents (Prince George's + Charles County)
**Who:** Students 16–18 at Title I schools, parents interested in college alternatives
**Where:** Instagram, TikTok, school counselor networks, church bulletins, community centers
**Message:** Earn AI credentials before graduation + employer pipeline + pay
**Tactic:** Student ambassador program + school counselor briefings

### Audience 4: Adult Learners (Career Changers, Returning Citizens)
**Who:** Adults 25–45 in AI growth corridors, workforce center clients
**Where:** Facebook, community partner networks, MDOL workforce centers
**Message:** Your experience + AI skills = career transformation
**Tactic:** Free info session at partner sites + peer testimonial videos

### Audience 5: Foundation and Government Funders
**Who:** Abell Foundation, Goldseker, GSF, TEDCO, MSDE
**Where:** Foundation portals + direct relationship (no cold outreach)
**Message:** Systems change + structural funding + community governance
**Tactic:** Tailored proposals + Bianca 1:1 meetings
