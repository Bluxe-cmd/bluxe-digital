## Year 2–3 Funding Shift

| Source | Year 1 | Year 2 | Year 3 |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Grants | 45% | 25% | 10% |
| CBA Contracts | 27% | 40% | 45% |
| Employer Fees | 18% | 25% | 30% |
| Government Contracts | 10% | 10% | 15% |
| **Total Target** | **$450K** | **$900K** | **$1.8M** |

**Strategy:** Use Year 1 grants to build evidence. Use evidence to win Year 2 CBA contracts and government contracts. By Year 3, grants are less than 10% of revenue.

---

## Nonprofit vs. For-Profit Decision

**Current recommendation:** Hybrid structure  
- **LLC or PBC (Public Benefit Corporation):** Operating entity for employer contracts, CBA contracts, training fees
- **501(c)(3) Nonprofit:** Fundraising arm for grants, VLT, GSF tax credits (some require nonprofit status)

**Reason:** Some funders (VLT, some foundations) require nonprofit status. But CBA contracts and employer fees are easier to capture in a for-profit entity.

→ See: [`../06-compliance-legal/nonprofit-structure.md`](../06-compliance-legal/nonprofit-structure.md)
