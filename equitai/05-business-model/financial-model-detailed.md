## Scenario Planning

### Scenario A: Optimistic (Base Case)
All revenue sources activate on schedule. CBA contract signed by Month 4. TEDCO approved by Month 3.  
**Year 1 Revenue: $471,000 | Net: +$135,600**

### Scenario B: Conservative (Plan For This)
CBA delayed to Month 6. TEDCO pending (use foundation grants as bridge). Government contract delayed to Year 2.  
**Year 1 Revenue: $280,000 | Net: +$20,000**

### Scenario C: Stress Test (Know Your Floor)
No CBA in Year 1. TEDCO denied. Only foundation grants + 1–2 employer contracts.  
**Year 1 Revenue: $150,000 | Net: -$20,000** (requires bridge funding or reduced costs)

**Decision Rule:**
- If Scenario C seems likely by Month 3 → Cut costs immediately (delay ops hire, reduce facilitator retainer)
- If Scenario B seems likely by Month 3 → Accelerate employer contract outreach; don't wait for government

## Financial Red Flags — Stop and Reassess If:

1. **Month 3 cash balance < $5,000** — something in the funding pipeline isn't landing. Call Abell + TEDCO immediately.
2. **No signed contracts by Month 5** — employer outreach isn't converting. Change approach or lower the ask.
3. **CBA conversation stalls with no developer meeting by Month 3** — need a different entry point (county officials, community coalition, different developer).
4. **Completion rate below 60%** — participant support model is failing. Add stipends, adjust schedule, bring in a case manager.
5. **Burn rate exceeds $35K/month in Months 1–3** — costs are running ahead of revenue. Cut before the cash runs out.
