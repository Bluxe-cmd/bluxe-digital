## Business Model Summary

EquitAI generates revenue through five interlocking streams designed to move from grant-dependent (Year 1) to structurally self-funded (Year 3) through contracts and earned revenue.

**The key insight:** Every revenue stream reinforces the others. A CBA contract generates learners → learners generate employment outcomes → outcomes unlock government contracts → government contracts fund more cohorts → more cohorts attract more employers → employers fund the next CBA negotiation.

---

## Revenue Stream Definitions

### Stream 1: CBA Training Contracts
**What:** Data center developers pay EquitAI to deliver AI literacy training as fulfillment of their Community Benefit Agreement workforce obligations.  
**Unit economics:** $2,400/participant/cohort (120-hour program)  
**Contract structure:** Annual training contract with developer; EquitAI delivers cohorts at partner sites  
**Year 1 target:** 1 contract × 50 participants = $120,000

### Stream 2: Employer Training Fees
**What:** Employers pay EquitAI to deliver customized AI upskilling for their current workforce.  
**Unit economics:** $1,800/employee (40-hour accelerated track) | $3,200/employee (80-hour deep track)  
**Contract structure:** Per-employee fee; quarterly billing; minimum cohort of 10  
**Year 1 target:** 3 employers × 15 employees avg × $1,800 = $81,000

### Stream 3: Government Workforce Contracts
**What:** MDOL, MSDE, or county workforce agencies contract with EquitAI to deliver training for eligible workforce populations.  
**Unit economics:** $3,500/participant (government contracts cover full cost including support services)  
**Contract structure:** Per-participant or per-cohort government contract; quarterly disbursement  
**Year 1 target:** 1 contract × 30 participants = $105,000

### Stream 4: Grant Funding
**What:** Foundation and government grants funding program development, early operations, and innovation.  
**Sources:** TEDCO ($50K), VLT ($25K), Foundations ($35K), GSF ($40K)  
**Year 1 target:** $150,000

### Stream 5: Program Fees (Individual Learners)
**What:** Adults who don't qualify for employer or government-funded programs pay a sliding-scale fee.  
**Unit economics:** $0–$500/participant (sliding scale; most subsidized)  
**Year 1 target:** Low — $15,000 (keep accessible in Year 1, scale in Year 2)

## Unit Economics (Per Learner)

### Full Cohort (120 Hours — Track A+B)
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Revenue per learner (CBA) | $2,400 |
| Revenue per learner (employer) | $1,800–$3,200 |
| Revenue per learner (government) | $3,500 |
| Cost per learner (variable) | $650–$900 |
| Gross margin per learner (CBA) | $1,500–$1,750 (63–73%) |
| Cost of facilitator (per cohort of 20) | $4,000–$6,000 |
| Materials cost per learner | $50–$100 |
| Space cost per learner | $50–$150 |

### Key Insight: The CBA model is the highest-margin pathway
- CBA: developer pays; community gets trained; EquitAI earns $1,500+ net per learner
- Government: highest gross revenue but slowest to contract and heaviest reporting
- Employer: fastest to close, good margins, repeat business potential

## Break-Even Analysis

**Monthly fixed costs:** ~$28,000  
**Break-even at CBA pricing:** 19 participants/month (at $2,400 each)  
**Break-even at government pricing:** 8 participants/month (at $3,500 each)  
**Break-even at blended pricing:** 12–14 participants/month

**Year 1 break-even timeline:** Month 6–7 (when CBA + employer + government revenue converges)

## Key Financial Risks and Mitigations

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| CBA contract delayed (developer slow) | Medium | High | Pursue 2–3 simultaneous developer conversations |
| TEDCO grant not approved | Low | Medium | Have Abell/Goldseker LOIs ready as backup |
| Government contract delayed | High | Medium | CBA + employer contracts can cover gap |
| Founder salary insufficient | High | Personal | Set aside savings; plan spouse/partner income bridge |
| Cohort attrition > 30% | Medium | Medium | Participant stipends ($100/cohort) reduce attrition; strong facilitator model |
| Curriculum not ready by Month 4 | Low | High | Track A (40 hours) should be ready Month 2; Track B can follow |
