## How to Find the Right Contact

For companies where you don't have a name:

1. **LinkedIn:** Search "[Company] + workforce development OR diversity recruiting OR community affairs + Maryland"
2. **Company website:** Look for: Community, Corporate Responsibility, HR, or Talent Acquisition sections
3. **Google:** "[Company name] Maryland workforce OR community OR CBA"
4. **Ask your network:** "Do you know anyone at [Company]?" — a warm intro beats cold outreach every time
5. **Events:** Maryland Chamber of Commerce, MEDA, PGCEDC events — meet people in person
