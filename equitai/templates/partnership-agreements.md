## TEMPLATE A: Employer Workforce Pipeline Agreement

---

# WORKFORCE PIPELINE PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT

**Between:**  
[Employer Name], a [entity type] ("Employer")  
AND  
EquitAI, LLC, a Maryland limited liability company ("EquitAI")

**Effective Date:** [Date]

---

### 1. PURPOSE

The purpose of this Agreement is to establish a workforce training and talent pipeline partnership between EquitAI and Employer, under which EquitAI will provide qualified, credentialed AI-literacy graduates to Employer for consideration for employment, and Employer will support EquitAI's training programs as described herein.

---

### 2. EQUITAI COMMITMENTS

EquitAI agrees to:

**2.1 Talent Pipeline**
- Deliver a minimum of [X] EquitAI graduates per [quarter / year] with current EquitAI credentials
- Provide Employer with graduate profiles, credentials, and portfolio projects
- Facilitate candidate introductions and scheduling of interviews

**2.2 Curriculum Alignment**
- Invite Employer to participate in EquitAI's Employer Advisory Council (quarterly, 1 hour)
- Incorporate Employer's feedback on in-demand skills in curriculum updates

**2.3 Reporting**
- Provide Employer with a semi-annual graduate placement report
- Track and share 6-month employment outcomes for Employer-hired graduates

---

### 3. EMPLOYER COMMITMENTS

Employer agrees to:

**3.1 Interview Guarantee**
- Interview every EquitAI graduate who applies for an open position at Employer, provided the graduate holds a current EquitAI credential and the role is within EquitAI's training scope

**3.2 Apprenticeship/Hiring Commitment**
- Provide a minimum of [X] apprenticeship or entry-level positions per [year] to EquitAI graduates
- Minimum starting wage: $[18.00] per hour
- Minimum apprenticeship duration: [6 months]

**3.3 Training Investment** (Optional — if employer is funding training)
- Employer agrees to pay EquitAI $[AMOUNT] per [quarter / year] as a workforce training investment
- This investment may qualify for Maryland workforce training tax credits (Employer responsible for confirming eligibility with Maryland Department of Commerce)

**3.4 Feedback**
- Provide EquitAI with hiring manager feedback within 30 days of each graduate interview
- Participate in one annual curriculum advisory meeting

---

### 4. TERM

This Agreement has an initial term of [1 year] from the Effective Date and renews automatically for successive one-year terms unless either party provides 60 days written notice.

---

### 5. CONFIDENTIALITY

Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of graduate personal information consistent with applicable law. EquitAI will not share individual graduate performance data with Employer without graduate consent.

---

## TEMPLATE B: Community College Partnership MOU

---

# MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING
# Workforce Training Partnership

**Between:**  
[College Name] ("College")  
AND  
EquitAI, LLC ("EquitAI")

**Date:** [Date]

---

### Purpose

This Memorandum of Understanding establishes the framework for collaboration between [College Name] and EquitAI to expand AI literacy workforce training opportunities for students and community members in [county/region].

---

### Goals of the Partnership

1. Expand access to AI workforce credentials for HS students and adult learners
2. Create articulation pathways between EquitAI credentials and [College] credit programs
3. Jointly pursue workforce development funding (EARN Maryland, MDOL, foundations)
4. Share resources and space to increase program reach

---

### College Commitments

The College agrees to:
- [ ] Provide classroom / lab space for [X] EquitAI cohorts per year at [cost / in-kind]
- [ ] Explore credit articulation for EquitAI Track B and Track D credentials
- [ ] Co-market EquitAI programs to workforce and continuing education students
- [ ] Designate a liaison to coordinate scheduling and logistics
- [ ] Participate as co-applicant on [EARN Maryland / MDOL] funding applications (if mutually agreed)

---

### EquitAI Commitments

EquitAI agrees to:
- [ ] Deliver [X] cohorts per year at the College site
- [ ] Provide curriculum documentation for credit articulation review
- [ ] Train facilitators to meet College standards for non-credit instruction (if applicable)
- [ ] Share outcome data with College for grant reporting
- [ ] Acknowledge College partnership in program materials and communications

---

- EquitAI will pay College $[AMOUNT] per cohort for space/resource use.
- College will pay EquitAI $[AMOUNT] for curriculum delivery under a separate contract.

---

### Term

This MOU is effective [date] through [date], renewable by mutual written agreement.

## TEMPLATE C: Community Organization Partnership Agreement

---

# COMMUNITY PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT

**Between:**  
[Organization Name] ("Partner")  
AND  
EquitAI, LLC ("EquitAI")

**Date:** [Date]

---

### Partnership Purpose

[Partner] and EquitAI agree to work together to bring AI literacy workforce training to the [neighborhood/community] served by [Partner], with the goal of providing local residents access to AI career credentials and employment opportunities.

---

### Partner Commitments

- Recruit [X] eligible participants per cohort from Partner's network and community
- Provide space at [address] for EquitAI cohort delivery ([X hours/week, schedule TBD])
- Promote EquitAI program to Partner's constituents through [newsletter / social media / events]
- Designate a point of contact for logistics coordination
- Provide participant support services (case management, childcare referral, etc.) consistent with Partner's existing programs

---

### EquitAI Commitments

- Deliver [X] cohorts per year at Partner's site
- Train and certify [X] community facilitators from Partner's community (if applicable)
- Share program outcomes with Partner for their own reporting
- Acknowledge Partner in program materials and press
- Pay Partner a coordination stipend of $[AMOUNT] per cohort (if applicable)

---

### Community Benefit

Both parties commit to ensuring that the program:
- Is free to participants (no tuition or participation fees)
- Is delivered in a culturally responsive manner
- Prioritizes community members with the greatest barriers to AI workforce access
- Is governed with input from community members

---

### Term

This Agreement is effective for [1 year] and may be renewed by mutual agreement.

## TEMPLATE D: Facilitator Contractor Agreement

---

# INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT
# EquitAI Program Facilitator

**Between:**  
EquitAI, LLC ("EquitAI")  
AND  
[Facilitator Name] ("Facilitator")

**Date:** [Date]

---

### Services

Facilitator agrees to deliver EquitAI [Track A / B / D] curriculum to a cohort of [X] participants at [location], for [X] sessions of [X] hours each, beginning [start date] and concluding [end date].

Facilitator agrees to:
- Prepare for and deliver all assigned sessions
- Track attendance and engagement for each session
- Submit weekly progress notes to EquitAI program coordinator
- Administer pre/post assessments per EquitAI protocol
- Maintain participant confidentiality
- Complete EquitAI facilitator certification before delivery begins (if not yet certified)

---

### Compensation

EquitAI agrees to pay Facilitator $[RATE] per hour for delivered sessions, plus $[RATE] per hour for preparation time (maximum [X] prep hours per session).

Payment: Within 15 business days of receiving Facilitator's submitted time records.

Facilitator is an independent contractor. No benefits, withholding, or employment relationship is created by this Agreement.

---

### Term

This Agreement covers the cohort specified above and terminates upon cohort completion, unless renewed for a subsequent cohort by mutual agreement.
