## Outreach Rules

1. **Personalize every email** — at minimum, replace all [bracketed fields]
2. **Research before sending** — know the person's name, role, and 1 thing specific to them
3. **One ask per email** — don't overwhelm with multiple asks
4. **Short is better** — if an email is longer than 200 words, cut it
5. **Follow up exactly once** — after 7 days, one follow-up. After that, move on.
6. **Track everything** — in your CRM or the table above
7. **Warm introductions beat cold emails** — always ask "do you know anyone at X?" before cold outreach
