# WORKFORCE TRAINING COMMUNITY BENEFIT AGREEMENT

**Between:**  
[Developer Entity Name], a [state] [entity type] ("Developer")  
AND  
EquitAI, LLC, a Maryland limited liability company ("EquitAI")  
AND  
[Community Organization Name], a Maryland nonprofit corporation ("Community Partner")

**Date:** [Date]  
**Project:** [Data Center Project Name and Address]

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Developer is developing a data center facility located at [address], [county], Maryland ("the Project");

WHEREAS, Developer recognizes the importance of community investment and workforce development as part of responsible development practice;

WHEREAS, EquitAI is a Maryland-based AI literacy workforce organization with experience delivering workforce training programs for high school students and adult career changers in [county];

WHEREAS, Community Partner is a community organization representing residents in the area of the Project;

WHEREAS, the parties desire to enter into this Agreement to define and formalize workforce training commitments associated with the Project;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants herein, the parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: TRAINING INVESTMENT COMMITMENT

### 1.1 Annual Training Investment
Developer agrees to pay EquitAI an annual training fee of $[AMOUNT] per year for [TERM] years ("the Training Investment"), beginning [START DATE].

**Payment schedule:** Quarterly, in equal installments of $[AMOUNT/4], due on [January 15 / April 15 / July 15 / October 15] of each calendar year.

**Total committed investment over [TERM] years:** $[TOTAL AMOUNT]

### 1.2 Use of Training Investment
EquitAI agrees to use the Training Investment exclusively for:
- Design, delivery, and administration of AI literacy workforce training cohorts
- Facilitator compensation and training
- Curriculum development and materials
- Participant support (stipends, materials, technology access)
- Program administration and reporting

### 1.3 Training Capacity
EquitAI agrees to enroll and serve a minimum of [NUMBER] participants per year ("Annual Training Target") using the Training Investment. Participants shall be prioritized from:
1. Residents within [X] miles of the Project
2. Households with income below [X]% of Area Median Income
3. Historically underrepresented populations in technology fields
4. Current PGCPS/[school district] high school students

---

## ARTICLE 2: APPRENTICESHIP AND HIRING COMMITMENTS

### 2.1 Apprenticeship Slots
Developer agrees to provide a minimum of [NUMBER] paid apprenticeship or entry-level employment positions per year for EquitAI graduates ("Apprenticeship Commitment"), for the [TERM] of this Agreement.

**Apprenticeship terms:**
- Duration: Minimum [6] months per apprentice
- Compensation: Minimum $[18.00] per hour
- Benefits: [Specify — health insurance, PTO, etc.]
- Training: On-the-job AI/data center operations training

### 2.2 Interview Guarantee
Developer agrees to conduct an employment interview with every EquitAI graduate who applies for an open position at the Project, provided the graduate holds a valid EquitAI Workforce Ready Certificate or Technical Certificate.

### 2.3 Local Hiring Preference
Developer agrees to give preference to EquitAI graduates and residents of [county] for Project operational jobs, consistent with applicable law.

---

## ARTICLE 3: CURRICULUM AND EMPLOYER INPUT

### 3.1 Curriculum Advisory Role
Developer shall have the opportunity to designate one representative to participate in EquitAI's Employer Advisory Council, which reviews curriculum content and provides input on in-demand skills.

### 3.2 Job Posting Notification
Developer shall notify EquitAI of any open positions at the Project within 14 days of posting, to enable EquitAI to refer qualified graduates.

---

## ARTICLE 4: COMMUNITY GOVERNANCE

### 4.1 Community Advisory Council
EquitAI shall constitute a Community Advisory Council ("CAC") that includes:
- At least [2] residents of the community surrounding the Project
- At least [1] representative from Community Partner
- At least [1] EquitAI program graduate
- At least [1] local employer representative

### 4.2 CAC Role
The CAC shall:
- Review EquitAI program outcomes annually
- Provide feedback on curriculum relevance and community fit
- Advise on any proposed changes to the training program
- Be notified of any material changes to this Agreement

---

## ARTICLE 5: REPORTING AND ACCOUNTABILITY

### 5.1 Annual Report
EquitAI shall provide Developer and Community Partner with an Annual Report by [date] each year, including:
- Total participants enrolled and completing the program
- Demographics of participants
- Employment outcomes (6-month follow-up data)
- Average wage at placement
- Number of apprenticeships provided by Developer
- Any curriculum changes
- Financial summary of Training Investment use

### 5.2 Community Meetings
The parties agree to hold at least one joint community meeting per year to present program outcomes to the community.

### 5.3 Audit Rights
Community Partner shall have the right, with 30 days written notice, to request an independent financial review of how Training Investment funds were used, not to exceed once per year.

---

## ARTICLE 6: TERM AND RENEWAL

### 6.1 Term
This Agreement shall have an initial term of [TERM] years from the Effective Date.

### 6.2 Renewal
This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one-year terms unless any party provides 90 days written notice of non-renewal.

### 6.3 Termination for Cause
Either party may terminate this Agreement for material breach, provided:
- Written notice of breach is provided
- The breaching party has 60 days to cure the breach
- If uncured, the non-breaching party may terminate

---

## ARTICLE 7: PAYMENT OBLIGATIONS ON TERMINATION

### 7.1 Developer Non-Performance
If Developer fails to make a scheduled Training Investment payment and does not cure within 30 days of notice, EquitAI shall be entitled to:
- The unpaid installment(s)
- Interest at [5]% per annum on unpaid amounts
- Reimbursement of reasonable attorney's fees in collection

### 7.2 EquitAI Non-Performance
If EquitAI fails to deliver the Annual Training Target for two consecutive years, Developer may reduce the Training Investment by a pro-rata amount or, if the failure is not cured in the following year, terminate this Agreement.

---

## ARTICLE 8: GENERAL PROVISIONS

### 8.1 Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Maryland.

### 8.2 Dispute Resolution
The parties agree to attempt mediation before initiating litigation. Mediation shall be conducted by a mutually agreed mediator in [county], Maryland.

### 8.3 Entire Agreement
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement of the parties regarding its subject matter and supersedes all prior agreements.

### 8.4 Amendment
This Agreement may be amended only by a written instrument signed by all parties.

### 8.5 Publicity
The parties may issue press releases regarding this Agreement with prior written consent of the other parties, not to be unreasonably withheld.
